## 4 Expert Tips for Using One Drop Rewind

### 1. Prep your skin:

One Drop Rewind works best when it’s layered on top of your toners, serums, and moisturizers…

So complete your regular skincare routine before applying this “quick fix” formula.

### 2. The Power of Precision:

When it comes to applying One Drop Rewind, precision is key. Utilize the soft-tipped brush provided to delicately layer the product over targeted areas – whether it’s crow’s feet, marionette lines, or stubborn forehead creases.

Remember — a little goes a long way, so start with a small amount of product and build your application as needed.

### 3. Take your time:

After applying One Drop Rewind, wait about 2-3 minutes for the formula to deliver the look and feel of tight, youthful skin before applying makeup. This ensures a beautifully smooth, flawless finish.

### 4. Reapply as needed:

One Drop Rewind is designed to give you visibly “ageless” skin for up to 8 hours, but we understand you may need your results to last longer than that.

If you need extended results, we recommend taking your One Drop Rewind bottle with you, so you can apply touch-ups as needed. After all, when you see how youthful and virtually lineless this formula makes your skin look — you won’t want to go a second without it.
